Weekly Events

Story Time at the Library, City Library Saturdays at 10:30 AM

Bring your kids and enjoy a story at the local library. Two different groups are held, one recomended for ages 10 and younger, and the other for 11 and older.

Library Book Club, City Library Fridays at 7:30 PM

Recomended for those 16 and older, this group meets weekly at the library for discussing and reading a book!

Major Events

5/30/22 Memorial Day Event, City Park ★

View an array a flags set up in remembrance of Memorial Day. Honor those who have served our country.

7/4/22 4th of July Picnic, City Park at 6 PM ★

Enjoy spending time with your family, while spending time with your neighbors! Bring your own food and watch the fireworks set up for the evening.

10/31/22 Halloween Trunk or Treat, City Park Parking Lot at 7 PM ★

Halloween is here and what better way to celebrate it than at the city park! Decorate your car and hand out candy, at this once a year event. Costume contest to be held, ages 18 and younger.
